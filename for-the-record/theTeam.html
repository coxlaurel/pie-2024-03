Budget and Materials

Team members from left to right: Nick, Annabelle, Julianne, Elías, and Marcellus



Nick worked on the drivetrain, turntable, wiring, and organization of wires and other electrical components in the lower interior of the box.

Annabelle worked on the box design, along with most other design and artistic choices such as box assembly, UI, and box decoration.

Julianne worked on electrical systems, most notably the sound system that converts the cartridge output to appropriate speaker input.

Elías worked on project management, documentation, organizing firmware, and writing software for the arduinos, digital display, and this website.

Marcellus worked on mechanical systems, primarily in creating a record arm that operates relative to the turntable, while allowing electrical connection to the cartridge.
