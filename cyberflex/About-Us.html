About Us

We were brought together as undergraduate students from Olin College of Engineering, an institution near the Boston area, for our final project because we were taking a class called Principles of Integrated Engineering! For our final project, the requirements are to create something with mechanical, electrical, and software aspects. We want to create something that was biomedical based and could help others down the line.
